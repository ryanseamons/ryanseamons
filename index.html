Most people have no idea what they want.

We are bad at predicting what will make us happy. This especially impacts work and family relationships.

The #1 regret of the dying is living life by the expectations of others vs living a life true to yourself. Too often our thoughts and actions are misaligned with our true identity and purpose.

The ability to figure out who you are, what you want, and how to ask for it is critical to ensure you experience lasting happiness in your marriage, in your work, and in your life.
